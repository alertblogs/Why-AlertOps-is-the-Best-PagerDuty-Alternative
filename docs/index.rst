Why AlertOps is the Best PagerDuty Alternative
========

 
When comparing PagerDuty alternatives, AlertOps stands out among the competition
====
We will compare AlertOps to PagerDuty in 3 broad areas:

Flexibility
====
On-call management
Whether your on-call management needs are basic or complex, AlertOps has a solution for you. Creating on-call schedules is simple whether there one person on-call, two or more people on-call, or even multiple teams on-call.

Silence alert noise
Every AlertOps integration, from web API to email integrations, features alert grouping to de-duplicate your alerts.
Advanced features allow you to do more, such as delay flapping alerts to allow time for the situation to self correct.

No code platform
Connect your tools to AlertOps using our pre-configured integrations or build your own integrations (inbound and outbound) using our open APIs without writing a single line of code.

Automation
====
Collaborate using your tools of choice
Take actions on an alert from MS Teams or Slack.

Escalations
Automatic escalations based on your on-call schedules. Expand the possibilities with Workflows and Escalation Rule.

Bi-directional service desk integrations
Seamless data flows and workflows integrate with your service desk tools.

Expert Guidance
